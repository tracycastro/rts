Welcome!

The Royale Theatre Society (RTS) is an independent 501(c)(3) non-profit organization that supports MacTheatre productions and enrichment programs through volunteer coordination and financial support.

As part of the McCallum Fine Arts Academy (MFAA), MacTheatre is comprised of student artists from across Austin who share a common drive, passion, and commitment to excellence in theatre arts. RTS support is crucial to sustaining this exemplary, tuition-free theatre program.
