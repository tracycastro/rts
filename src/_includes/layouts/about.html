Get Involved

All parents are invited to participate in RTS activities and become active supporters of MacTheatre. There are a wide variety of opportunities available to get involved. You can take on a part-time role for the season, or volunteer at performances! We help coordinate:

  • Publicity
  • Box Office
  • Concessions
  • Cast & Crew Meals
  • Ushers
  • Playbills
  • Arts Festival and Competition Travel
  • Scholarships

Support MacTheatre

RTS cultivates private funding by working with individual donors, local businesses, and foundations. Our supporters enable us to sustain and grow performance and enrichment opportunities that are available to all McCallum students. RTS funding supports:

  • Production expenses (e.g., scenery, costumes, concessions, etc.)
  • Creative fees for guest directors, choreographers, and music directors
  • Supplemental classes in dance, voice, and acting
  • Enrichment program scholarships
  • Participation in arts festivals and competitions
