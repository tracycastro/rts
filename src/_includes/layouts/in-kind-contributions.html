Contributor Benefits

  • Your logo with a link to your website will be included on the RTS supporters' page for the remainder of the season.
  • Your logo will be highlighted in the post-show email sent to all of the patrons of the show that the contribution applied to.

When the value of your contributions totals $500 or more in a single school semester, you will receive the benefits below during the following semester (half-season):

  • Your color logo on the back of the playbills for two shows.
  • Your logo with a link to your website on the RTS home page.
  • Your logo with a link to your website on the RTS supporters page as a lead producer.
  • Your logo with a link to your website on six promotional emails sent throughout the semester to ~5000 continuously updated email addresses of patrons from recent shows.
  • Your logo with a link to your website on two post-show emails sent to the patrons of each show.
  • A pass code for up to four tickets to a show.
