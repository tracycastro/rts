Align your brand with MacTheatre! Sponsoring our 2022/2023 season will help you gain visibility in the community and achieve your civic engagement and social responsibility goals. MacTheatre sponsors are essential to sustaining a high quality, tuition-free theatre program and developing the next generation of performing artists in Central Texas.

To learn more, contact development@macrts.org.

Season Sponsorships

MacTheatre reaches nearly 10,000 people through our productions each season. Our season sponsorship benefits include logo placement in our playbills, lobby, website, social media, and promotional emails that reach thousands of our devoted MacTheatre patrons throughout the season, as well as exclusive ticket passes. Sponsorship packages can be purchased here.

Sponsorship levels begin at $500.

Concessions Sponsorship

All proceeds from MacTheatre concessions go to the RTS scholarship fund, which is used to provide scholarships for student production fees, enrichment programs, and college. By offsetting our concessions costs, our concessions sponsor helps to ensure that all McCallum students, regardless of their financial circumstances, are able to participate in high quality theatre productions and enrichment programs. Contact us to learn more about how you can provide equal access to a performing arts education.

Sponsorship levels begin at $2500.

Tech Theatre Sponsorship

Austin is the top tech town in the country, and MacTheatre has a top notch tech theatre department to match. In 2022, we were Heller Award nominees for best lighting design, best scenic design, and best technical execution, and our students were honored with multiple UIL technical theatre accolades. Our tech theatre sponsors ensure that we continue to have state-of-the-art theatre technology in the classroom and in our productions. Contact us to find out how you can support the next generation of tech theatre artists.

Sponsorship levels begin at $5000, and may be spread over two years.
