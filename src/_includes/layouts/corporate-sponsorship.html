Season Sponsorships

MacTheatre reaches nearly 10,000 people through our productions each season. Our season sponsorship benefits include logo placement in our playbills, lobby, website, social media, and promotional emails that reach thousands of our devoted MacTheatre patrons throughout the season, as well as exclusive ticket passes.

Sponsorship levels begin at $500.

Program Sponsorships

MacTheatre program sponsors help fund our scholarship and enrichment programs, ensuring that all McCallum students, regardless of their financial circumstances, are able to participate in high quality theatre productions and enrichment programs. Contact us to learn more about how you can provide equal access to a performing arts education.

Sponsorship levels begin at $2500.

Tech Theatre Sponsorships

Austin is the top tech town in the country, and MacTheatre has a top notch tech theatre department to match. In 2022, MacTheatre’s production of Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat won the Heller Award for Best Technical Execution, and our one-act production of All The Way Home took home four UIL technical theatre accolades. Our tech theatre sponsors ensure that we continue to have state-of-the-art theatre technology in our classrooms and productions. Contact us to find out how you can support the next generation of tech theatre artists.

Sponsorship levels begin at $5000, and may be spread over two years.
