MacTheatre and the Royale Theatre Society believe that all students, regardless of their financial circumstances, should have access to high quality theatre productions and programs. No student will be prevented from participating in theatre because of an inability to pay.

Production Fee Scholarships

Production Fees (also known as Fair Share Fees) are annual fees that are required of all performance theatre majors, technical theatre majors, musical certificate students, and any other student participating in a production. Learn More.

If you would like to request a scholarship to cover the cost of your production fees, please fill out the application below. Applications are kept confidential and are seen only by the RTS Scholarships Committee chair.

Senior Scholarships

The Royale Theatre Society provides partial scholarships to seniors who have participated in MacTheatre during their time at McCallum. To request an application for a scholarship, please contact our scholarships committee chair at scholarships@macrts.org.
