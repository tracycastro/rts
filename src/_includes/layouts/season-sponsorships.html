Season Sponsorships

Align your brand with MacTheatre! Sponsoring our 2022/2023 season will help you gain visibility in the community and achieve your civic engagement and social responsibility goals. MacTheatre sponsors are essential to sustaining a high quality, tuition-free theatre program and developing the next generation of performing artists in Central Texas.

Questions? Contact development@macrts.org

Additional Information

MacTheatre produces four shows each season, with a combined audience of 8,000 to 10,000 patrons.

Logo/Profile Links – Logos and profile pictures on the macrts.org site will link to the web page of the sponsor’s choice. See our home page and our sponsor page for examples.

Post-Production Emails – After the run of each show, an email is sent to all the patrons of the show.

Promotional Emails – Leading up to each show, three separate publicity emails are sent to ~5000 continuously updated email addresses of patrons from recent shows.

Social Shout-Outs – MacTheatre will provide at least two group social shout-outs, one in the fall and one in the spring, that include all season sponsors, on Facebook and/or Instagram. Shout-out design is at the discretion of MacTheatre.

Tickets – You will receive a pass code that allows you to comp up to four or eight tickets (depending on your sponsorship level) for the season. The pass code may be used for tickets for any combination of performances and shows, up to your ticket limit.

Employee/Client Tickets – Season sponsors may contact development@macrts.org for up to eight comp tickets for clients and employees for the season.
