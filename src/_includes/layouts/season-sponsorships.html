Additional Benefits

Playbill and Lobby

MacTheatre produces four shows each season, two musicals and two plays. Playbill and lobby benefits are included for all shows. Playbill ads can be submitted here after your sponsorship purchase. Your logos and profile on the RTS website will link to your company’s website. See our home page for examples.

Promotional Emails

Leading up to each show, three promotional emails are sent to ~5000 continuously updated email addresses of patrons from recent shows. These emails include lead producer logos. After the run of each show, a post-show email is sent to all patrons of that show. These emails include all sponsor logos.

Social Media

MacTheatre will provide at least one group social shout-out for lead producers each semester on Facebook and/or Instagram . Shout-out design is at the discretion of MacTheatre.

Pass Code and Tickets

Sponsors receive a pass code that allows them to comp up to four or eight tickets for the season. The pass code may be used for tickets for any combination of performances and shows, up to the ticket limit designated by the sponsorship level. Lead producers may contact development@macrts.org for up to eight additional comp tickets for clients and employees throughout the season.
